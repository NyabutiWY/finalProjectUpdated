Basil (Ocimum basilicum L.), belonging to the Lamiaceae family, is an annual spicy plant generally utilized as a flavouring agent for food. It's particularly appreciated in cuisine for its distinctive aroma. Basil is mainly used as a spice but is also considered a main ingredient in certain preparations, such as “Pesto Genovese”.
